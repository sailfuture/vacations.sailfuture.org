About the SailFuture Academy Scholarship Endowment

Each $30,000 scholarship will ensure that (1) student a year will be able to attend tuition free in perpetuity.

You will be matched with a student each year, providing you with an opportunity to see the impact of your gift over a lifetime. Each endowed scholarship is $30,000 and is payable over three years. Each named scholarship of $30,000 will be invested as an endowment to form a $6M scholarship fund. The annual returns of the fund will grant the $6,500 scholarship needed for 100 students per year.
