Winter 2024-2025 Destinations

Bahamian Archipelago & Caribbean Lesser Antilles Fall Expedition

This fall, immerse yourself in the beauty and adventure of these tropical paradises, where every stop offers a new and captivating experience. Whether you're snorkeling in crystal-clear waters, hiking through verdant rainforests, or simply relaxing on sun-kissed beaches, this expedition promises a perfect blend of excitement and tranquility.

Destinations & Locations

Bahamian Archipelago

Bahamian Treasures Voyage

Sail through the turquoise waters of the Bahamas, exploring secluded cays, vibrant coral reefs, and historic shipwrecks. Experience the warm hospitality and rich culture of the islands, from bustling Nassau to the serene Exumas.

Caribbean Lesser Antilles

Explore Antigua, Barbuda, Montserrat, and Guadeloupe

Set sail to the captivating islands of Antigua, Barbuda, Montserrat, and Guadeloupe. Antigua boasts 365 stunning beaches, while Barbuda offers untouched pink sand shores. Montserrat's volcanic landscape invites exploration, and Guadeloupe enchants with lush rainforests and vibrant Creole culture. Each island offers a unique slice of paradise.
