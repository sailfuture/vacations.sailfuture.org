Summer, Fall, and Winter 2024 Destinations

Highlight Destinations & Must-See Locations Available for Booking

Explore Antigua, Barbuda, Montserrat, and Guadeloupe

Set sail to the captivating islands of Antigua, Barbuda, Montserrat, and Guadeloupe. Antigua boasts 365 stunning beaches, while Barbuda offers untouched pink sand shores. Montserrat's volcanic landscape invites exploration, and Guadeloupe enchants with lush rainforests and vibrant Creole culture. Each island offers a unique slice of paradise.

Trinidad and Tobago

Experience the vibrant twin islands of Trinidad and Tobago. Trinidad offers bustling cities, lush rainforests, and spectacular bird sanctuaries, while Tobago boasts serene beaches and rich marine life. Enjoy a perfect blend of culture, adventure, and relaxation.

Saint Lucia & Grenada

Discover the allure of Saint Lucia and Grenada, two Caribbean gems. In Saint Lucia, hike the iconic Pitons and explore hidden waterfalls. In Grenada, known as the "Spice Isle," dive into crystal-clear waters and savor aromatic spice plantations. Both islands promise adventure, relaxation, and unforgettable experiences.

Bahamian Archipelago

Sail through the turquoise waters of the Bahamas, exploring secluded cays, vibrant coral reefs, and historic shipwrecks. Experience the warm hospitality and rich culture of the islands, from bustling Nassau to the serene Exumas.
