Summer in New England & The North Atlantic Coast

There are few better places to spend the summer than the North Atlantic Coast. Discover the captivating charm of new england, sail with the whales in Nova Scotia, and explore the magical Maine Coast.

North Atlantic Coast

Maine & Coastal Towns: Discover the Beauty of Acadia

Where steady breezes, glittering bays, and literally thousands of islands make Penobscot Bay one of the most revered cruising grounds on earth. We have complete liberty to explore secret anchorages, remote fishing villages, and charming gallery towns. Come touch this culturally distinct, pristine and physically breathtaking part of America.

North Atlantic Coast

Martha's Vineyard Island & Wine Sunset Sails

Depart from Newport, Rhode Island and sail to Block Island, Nantucket, Martha’s Vineyard & Cape Cod, beloved for its rocky coastline dotted with classic lighthouses, quaint colonial towns, and quiet beaches fringed by archetypal dunes flecked with long grasses.

North Atlantic Coast

Nova Scotia Whale-Watching Expedition

The coast of Nova Scotia remains the finest accessible cruising ground in North America. Explore the busy, historic harbors—among them Halifax, Lunenburg, and Louisbourg—and many, many quiet, pristine, secluded anchorages where you will not see another boat.
