Chart Your Own Adventure through the Caribbean & Lesser Antilles

Choose your own journey through the Caribbean and Lesser Antilles. From summer to winter, embark on an unforgettable adventure across the Caribbean Sea. Guests have the unique opportunity to select from any of the four stunning destinations during our available charter weeks. Revel in vibrant island cultures, explore hidden gems, and unwind on the pristine beaches of this tropical paradise. Experience the magic of the Caribbean in every season.

Saint Lucia & Grenada

Discover the allure of Saint Lucia and Grenada, two Caribbean gems. In Saint Lucia, hike the iconic Pitons and explore hidden waterfalls. In Grenada, known as the "Spice Isle," dive into crystal-clear waters and savor aromatic spice plantations. Both islands promise adventure, relaxation, and unforgettable experiences.
