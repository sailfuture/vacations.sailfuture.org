Overview

A Unique Way to Support SailFuture
While Traveling the World

Vacations with Purpose is an innovative approach to travel and philanthropy. It allows supporters of SailFuture to embark on a sailing vacation aboard the S/Y Whatever It Takes, where they can enjoy the beauty and thrill of sailing, knowing that their journey contributes directly to the organization's mission. This concept is more than just a holiday; it's an opportunity to be part of something bigger, to make a difference while exploring some of the most stunning destinations in the world.

The S/Y Whatever It Takes is not just any vessel; it's the newest flagship of SailFuture, a beautifully crafted yacht designed for both purpose and pleasure. When it's not engaged in its primary mission of serving SailFuture's population, this magnificent vessel is available for charter. It represents a perfect harmony of luxury and purpose, providing a one-of-a-kind experience for its guests.
