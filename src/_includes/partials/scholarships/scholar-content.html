About the SailFuture Academy
Scholarship Endowment

The SailFuture Academy Scholarship Endowment is a initiative designed to support the education of at-risk students in St. Petersburg. Each $30,000 scholarship provides tuition-free education for one student annually in perpetuity. Donors are matched with a student each year, allowing them to witness the lasting impact of their generosity.

Each $30,000 scholarship contributes to a larger $6 million scholarship fund, and the annual returns are used to grant $6,500 scholarships to 100 students every year.

Scholarships to the SailFuture Academy are more than just financial aid; they offer life-changing opportunities for these students.

By attending SailFuture Academy, students gain access to unique educational experiences that nurture independent thinking and entrepreneurial skills, ultimately guiding them towards becoming successful and self-reliant high school graduates.
