A Unique Way to Support SailFuture
While Traveling the World

For just 10-weeks a year, S/Y Whatever it Takes will be available for you to join us for a week-long vacation in one of the most beautiful places in the world in the most ideal time to vacation there.

These Vacations with Purpose take place in between student expeditions and 100% of your donation supports our critical work to help at-risk youth transform their lives through adventure therapy and wraparound programming.
