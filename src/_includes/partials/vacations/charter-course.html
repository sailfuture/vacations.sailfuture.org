Accomodations

A Unique Way to Support SailFuture While Traveling the World

For just 10 weeks a year, our 100' sailing yacht S/Y 'Whatever It Takes' is open for week-long vacations in some of the most beautiful places in the world. Guests may customize their vacation experience to make it as adventurous or luxurious as desired.

These Vacations with Purpose take place in between student expeditions and 100% of your donation supports our critical work to help at-risk youth transform their lives through adventure therapy and wraparound programming.
