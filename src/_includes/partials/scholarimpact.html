About the SailFuture Academy
Scholarship Endowment

The SailFuture Academy Scholarship Endowment is a remarkable initiative designed to support the education of at-risk students in St. Petersburg. Each $30,000 scholarship provides tuition-free education for one student annually in perpetuity. Donors are matched with a student each year, allowing them to witness the lasting impact of their generosity. The structure of the endowment is such that each $30,000 scholarship contributes to a larger $6 million scholarship fund. This fund is invested, and the annual returns are used to grant $6,500 scholarships to 100 students every year. This sustainable model ensures ongoing support for the education of at-risk youth.

Your scholarships is more than just financial aid; it is a life-changing opportunity. By attending SailFuture Academy, students gain access to unique educational experiences that nurture independent thinking and entrepreneurial skills, ultimately guiding them towards becoming successful and self-reliant high school graduates.
